Privacy Policy

Welcome to Whole AI. We prioritize your privacy above all else. Our platform is built on a Zero-Knowledge Architecture, ensuring that your intellectual property remains exclusively yours.

1. Code Data Storage

We NEVER store your source code on our permanent servers. When you run a modernizer or bug hunter session, the code is processed in volatile memory and purged immediately after the AI response is generated.

2. Encryption & Security

All data transmissions between your browser and our engine are protected by high-grade 256-bit SSL encryption.

3. Local Experience

Chat history is stored locally in your browser's encrypted cache.

4. Lifetime Guarantee

Whole AI is committed to maintaining this strict privacy standard for the lifetime of our service.

FAQ

Q1: What is Whole AI? expand_more
Whole AI is an AI-powered coding assistant that helps you optimize, debug, fix, and build app code or web code with mobile preview or zip download projects across multiple programming languages.
Q2: How many credits do I get per day? expand_more
Free Plan = 12 credits/day (resets daily automatically).
Q3: Can I get extra credits for free? expand_more
Yes! Watch a 15-second ad = 2 extra credits. You can do this up to 3 times per day (max 6 extra credits/day).
Q4: Which languages are free? expand_more
Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, PHP, C++, C#, Java, SQL — all always free ✅
Q5: Which languages need Pro? expand_more
Flutter, Go, Kotlin — Pro plan only 🔒
Q6: What are the paid plans? expand_more
Pro — $2 per 2 months → 50 credits/day + all languages + fast processing

Heavy Pro — $5 per year → 200 credits/day + ultra quality + ultra speed
Q7: Does Free Plan expire? expand_more
No! Free Plan has no expiry — lifetime access ✅
Q8: What are the 8 AI modes? expand_more
Modernizer, Bug Hunter, Quick Fixer, Security, Everything AI, AI Assistant, Build Web, Build App
Q9: Can I cancel anytime? expand_more
Yes — Upgrade anytime. Cancel anytime. No hidden fees.
Q10: How do I delete my account? expand_more
Go to Profile → scroll down → tap "Delete My Account" (red button).
